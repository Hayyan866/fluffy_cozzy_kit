Cat Care Tips

Feeding

Provide a balanced diet with high-quality cat food suitable for adult cats. Ensure fresh water is always available.

Grooming

Brush your cat weekly to reduce shedding and prevent hairballs. Check ears and trim nails regularly.

Health

Schedule annual vet visits for vaccinations and check-ups. Watch for signs of illness, like changes in appetite or behavior.
