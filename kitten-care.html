Kitten Care Tips

Feeding

Feed your kitten high-quality kitten food 3-4 times daily. Use small portions and ensure fresh water is always available.

Socialization

Gently handle your kitten daily to build trust. Introduce them to new people, sounds, and environments gradually.

Health

Schedule vet visits for vaccinations and deworming. Monitor for signs of illness, such as lethargy or diarrhea.
