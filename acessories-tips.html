Accessory Usage Tips

Using Toys

Engage your cat with interactive toys like feather wands or laser pointers for 10-15 minutes daily to promote exercise and mental stimulation.

Choosing Beds

Place cat beds in quiet, warm areas. Ensure they’re washable and sized appropriately for your cat’s comfort.

Maintaining Litter Boxes

Scoop litter boxes daily and clean weekly with mild soap. Place in a low-traffic area to encourage consistent use.
