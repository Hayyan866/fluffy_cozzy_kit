Feeding Tips

General Feeding

Provide a balanced diet with high-quality cat food. Ensure fresh water is always available and avoid feeding human foods that may be harmful.

Kitten Feeding

Feed kittens 3-4 times daily with kitten-specific food. Use small portions to support their growth and monitor their weight.

Adult Cat Feeding

Feed adult cats twice daily with portions based on their weight and activity level. Consult a vet for tailored dietary needs.
